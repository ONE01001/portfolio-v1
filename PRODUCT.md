# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Primary audience is tech recruiters looking for high-quality frontend talent. Secondary audience is the developer community on YouTube (portfolio showcase). The user wants to demonstrate technical depth and creative experimentation.

## Product Purpose
A high-fidelity developer portfolio that serves as a living demonstration of frontend mastery, interactive design, and AI-assisted development. It aims to secure career opportunities while establishing a unique personal brand identity.

## Brand Personality
Minimalist, Experimental, Dark, and Playful. The tone is "expertly confident" but not corporate. It should feel like a high-end personal project that values craft and unique interaction over generic templates.

## Anti-references
- **Generic SaaS/Corporate:** Avoid the "startup" look (clean white grids, rounded primary blue buttons, standard inter fonts).
- **Gamer/Neon Overdrive:** Avoid excessive "RGB" or neon-on-black aesthetics that feel immature or overstimulating.

## Design Principles
- **Practice What You Preach:** Every interactive element (ASCII, GSAP, Framer Motion) must be perfectly executed to prove technical skill.
- **Experimental Minimalism:** Keep the UI clean (minimalist) but introduce "weird" or unique interactions (experimental) that surprise the user.
- **Personal over Professional:** Favor personality and "self-representation" over standard professional templates.
- **Interactive Narrative:** Design for discovery (like the Secret Page) to reward curious visitors.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Supports reduced motion (via `useReducedMotion`).
- Dark-mode primary focus, ensuring high contrast for technical readability.
